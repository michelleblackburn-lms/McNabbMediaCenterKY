Meet Mrs. Blackburn

Welcome to the library! Yes, it’s run by me, your friendly middle school librarian (professional book matchmaker, bounty hunter of overdue books, part-time tech support, and full-time chaos coordinator).

I spend my days helping students find books they swear they won’t like…until they do, troubleshooting mysterious Chromebook issues, and reminding everyone that yes, library books do eventually need to be returned.

I believe the right book can change your whole day, libraries should never be boring, 80’s music makes everything better, and snacks should absolutely be allowed in the library (especially if you’re sharing).

If you need a book recommendation, research help, or just a place to exist for a minute, you’ve found your spot.

My philosophy centers on creating a welcoming, student-centered library environment that encourages curiosity, literacy, creativity, and lifelong learning.
