Makerspace

Our makerspace is a collaborative, hands-on learning environment located within the library where students use tools and creativity to turn ideas into projects. It combines STEM/STEAM principles with tinkering and innovation to encourage problem-solving, creativity, and teamwork.

Students may explore activities involving robotics, engineering challenges, crafting supplies, electronics, coding, and design thinking.

Beanstack Reading Program

Beanstack is a digital reading platform designed to motivate students to read independently through reading challenges, badges, and goal tracking. It helps build a positive reading culture while encouraging students to develop lifelong reading habits.
